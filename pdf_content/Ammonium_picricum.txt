 Ammonium picricum  
 (pictrate of ammonia)  
 * A remedy for malarial fever and neuralgias and so-called, bilious headaches.  
 * Pain in occiput and mastoid region.  
 * Whooping cough.  
 Head  
 - Periodical neuralgia in right side of occiput; boring extends to ear, orbit, and jaw.  
 - Vertigo on rising.  
 - Periodic bilious headaches. [Sanguin.]  
 Dose  
 - Third trituration.
