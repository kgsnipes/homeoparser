 Oleum santali  
 (oil of sandalwood)  
 * The action in the urinary and sexual spheres is most utilizable, especially in gonorrhoea.  
 * It is also a stimulating, disinfectant expectorant.  
 * Two or three drops on sugar will frequently relieve the hacking cough, when but little 
sputum is expectorated.  
 Male  
 - Painful erections; swelling of the prepuce.  
 - Thick, yellowish, muco-purulent discharge.  
 - Deep pain in perineum.  
 Urine  
 - Frequent, burning, smarting swelling, and redness of meatus.  
 - Stream small and slow.  
 - Acute aching in kidney region.  
 - Sensation of a ball pressing against the urethra; worse, standing.  
 - Gleet, with profuse, thick discharge; chronic cystitis.  
 Dose  
 - Two to ten m. in capsules.
