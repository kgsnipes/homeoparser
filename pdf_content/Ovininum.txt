 Ovininum  
 (ovarian extract)  
 * Suffering following excision of the ovaries.  
 * Climacteric disturbances generally.  
 * Ovarian cysts.  
 * Cutaneous disorders and acne rosacea.  
 * Prurigo.  
 Relationship  
 - Compare : Orchitinum-Testicular Extract- (after ovariotomy, sexual weakness, senile 
decay).  
 Dose  
 - Low triturations.
