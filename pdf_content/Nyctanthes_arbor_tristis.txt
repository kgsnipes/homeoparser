 Nyctanthes arbor tristis  
 (paghala-malli - sad tree)  
 * Bilious and obstinate remittent fever; sciatica; rheumatism.  
 * Constipation of children.  
 Head  
 - Anxious and restless; dull headache.  
 - Tongue coated.  
 Stomach  
 - Burning sensation, better cold application.  
 - Thirst, better vomiting.  
 Abdomen  
 - Tenderness of liver.  
 - Profuse, bilious stool, with nausea.  
 - Constipation.  
 Fever  
 - Thirst, before and during chill and heat; better vomiting as close of chill; sweat no marked.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture, drop doses.
