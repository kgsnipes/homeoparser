 Verbena officinalis  
 (blue vervain)  
 * Affects the skin and nervous system.  
 * Nervous depression, weakness and irritation and spasms.  
 * Promotes the absorption of blood and allays pain in bruises.  
 * Vesicular erysipelas.  
 * Passive congestion and intermittent fever.  
 * One of the remedies for Poison-oak.  
 * Epilepsy, insomnia, mental exhaustion.  
 * In epilepsy, it brightens up the patient's mental powers and helps the constipation.  
 Dose  
 - Single dose of the tincture.  
 - In epilepsy must be continued for a long time.  
 - Verbena in the form of a tea as a diuretic drink is used by Vannier (Paris) to aid elimination 
in tubercular therapy.
