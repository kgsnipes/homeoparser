 Ammonium phosphoricum  
 (phosphate of ammonia)  
 * A remedy for chronic gouty patients uric acid diathesis, indicated in bronchitis and 
nodosities of the joints of the fingers and backs of the hands.  
 * Facial paralysis.  
 * Pain in shoulder-joint.  
 * Tightness around chest.  
 * Heaviness of limbs, unsteady, tottering gait.  
 * Coldness from least draft of air.  
 Head  
 - Sneezing with excessive running from nose and eyes, only in morning.  
 Respiratory  
 - Deep rough cough with greenish expectoration.  
 Urine  
 - Rose-colored sediment.  
 Dose  
 - Third decimal trituration.
