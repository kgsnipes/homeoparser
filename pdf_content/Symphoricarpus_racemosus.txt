 Symphoricarpus racemosus  
 (snowberry)  
 * This drug is highly recommended for the persistent vomiting of pregnancy.  
 * Gastric disturbances, fickle appetite, nausea, waterbrash, bitter taste.  
 * Constipation.  
 * Nausea during menstruation.  
 * Nausea, worse any motion. Averse to all food. Better, lying on back.  
 Dose
