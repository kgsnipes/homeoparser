 Damiana  
 (damiana)  
 * Said to be of use in sexual neurasthenia; impotency.  
 * Sexual debility from nervous prostration.  
 * Incontinence of old people.  
 * Chronic prostatic discharge.  
 * Renal and cystic catarrh; frigidity of females.  
 * Aids the establishment of normal menstrual flow in young girls.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture and fluid extract-ten- to forty-drops doses.
