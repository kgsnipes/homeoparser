 Fuchsinum  
 (a coloring substance used in adulteration of wine)  
 * Produces redness of ears, deep red discoloration of mouth swollen gums, with burning and 
tendency to salivation; deep red urine, albuminous, and light red, profuse diarrhoea, with 
abdominal pains.  
 * Cortical substance of kidneys degenerated.  
 * Useful in cortical nephritis with albuminuria.  
 Dose  
 - 6x to 30th potency.
