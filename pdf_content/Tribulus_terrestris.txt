 Tribulus terrestris  
 (ikshugandha)  
 * An East Indian drug useful in urinary affections, especially dysuria, and in debilitated states 
of the sexual organs, as expressed in seminal weakness, ready emissions and impoverished 
semen.  
 * Prostatitis, calculous affections and sexual neurasthenia.  
 * It meets the auto-traumatism of masturbation correcting the emissions and spermatorrhoea.  
 * Partial impotence caused by overindulgence of advancing age, or when accompanied by 
urinary symptoms, incontinence, painful micturition, etc.  
 Dose  
 - Ten to twenty drops of the tincture three times daily.
