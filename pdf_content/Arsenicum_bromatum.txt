 Arsenicum bromatum  
 (bromide of arsenic)  
 * Has proven a great anti-psoric and anti-syphilitic remedy.  
 * Herpetic eruptions, syphilitic excrescences, glandular tumors and indurations, carcinoma, 
locomotor ataxia, and obstinate intermittents, and diabetes are all greatly influenced by this 
preparation.  
 Face  
 - Acne rosacea, with violet papules on nose; worse in the spring.  
 - Acne in young people.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture, two to four drops daily in water.  
 - In diabetes, three drops three times a day in a glass of water.
