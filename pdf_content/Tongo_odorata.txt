 Tongo odorata  
 (seeds of coumarouna -a tree in guiana)  
 Useful in neuralgia; pertussis.  
 Head  
 - Tearing pain in supra-orbital nerve, with heat and throbbing pain in head and epiphora.  
 - Confused, especially the occiput, with somnolence and a sort of intoxication.  
 - Trembling in right upper lid.  
 - Coryza; nose stopped, must breathe through mouth.  
 Extremities  
 - Tearing pains in hip-joints, femur, and knee, especially left side.  
 Relationship  
 - Melilotus. Anthoxanthum, Asperula, and Tonga contain Coumarin, the active principle.  
 - Compare them in hay-fever; also, Trifol.; Napth.; Sabad.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture and lower potencies.
