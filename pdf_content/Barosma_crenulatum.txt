 Barosma crenulatum  
 (buchu)  
 * Marked specific effects on genito-urinary system; muco-purulent discharges.  
 * Irritable bladder, with vesical catarrh; prostatic disorders.  
 * Gravel.  
 * Leucorrhoea.  
 Relationship  
 - Compare : Copaiva; Thuja; Populus; Chimaph.  
 - See Diosma.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture or tea from leaves.
