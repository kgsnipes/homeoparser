 Brachyglottis repens  
 (puka-puka)  
 * Fluttering sensation. [Caladium.] Kidney and bladder symptoms predominate.  
 * Produces symptoms of albuminuria.  
 * Itching in ears and nostrils.  
 * Bright's disease.  
 * Oppression of chest.  
 * Writer's cramp.  
 Abdomen  
 - Feeling as if something rolling about.  
 - Fluttering in region of ovary.  
 Urinary  
 - Pressure in neck of bladder; urging to urinate.  
 - Sense of swashing in bladder.  
 - Soreness in urethra; feeling as if urine could not be retained.  
 - Urine contains mucous corpuscles and epithelium, albumen and casts.  
 Extremities  
 - Cramp in fingers, thumb, and wrist when writing - soreness extending along flexor carpi 
ulnaris.  
 Relationship  
 - Compare : Apis; Helonias; Merc. cor.; Plumbum.  
 Dose  
 - Third potency.
