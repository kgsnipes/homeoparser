 Dolichos pruriens  
 (cowhage)  
 * A right-sided medicine, with pronounced liver and skin symptoms.  
 * A general intense itching without eruption.  
 * Exalted nervous sensibility.  
 * Senile pruritus.  
 * Haemorrhoidal diathesis.  
 Throat  
 - Pain in throat, worse swallowing, below right angle of jaw, as if splinter were imbedded 
vertically.  
 - Pain in gums prevents sleep.  
 Abdomen  
 - Colic from getting feet wet.  
 - Constipation, with intense itching; bloated abdomen.  
 - White stools.  
 - Swelling of liver.  
 - Haemorrhoids, with burning sensation.  
 Skin  
 - Intense itching, with no swelling or rash; worse across shoulders, also about elbows and 
knees and hairy parts.  
 - Jaundice.  
 - Yellow in spots; itching excessively at night.  
 - Herpes zoster. [Ars.]  
 Modalities  
 - Worse, at night, scratching, right side.  
 Relationship  
 - Compare : Rhus; Bell.; Hep.; Nit. ac.; Fagopyr.  
 Dose  
 - Sixth potency.  
 - Tincture, drop doses, in haemorrhoids.
