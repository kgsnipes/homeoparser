 Opuntia ficus indica  
 (prickly pear)  
 * Diarrhoea, with nausea.  
 * Feels as if bowels were settled down in lower abdomen.  
 * Sick feeling in lower third of abdomen.  
 * Enteroptosis with loose and frequent evacuations.  
 Relationship  
 - Compare ; Chaparra amargosa (which Mexican physicians laud as a specific in chronic 
diarrhoea).  
 - Ricinus communis (diarrhoea, dysentery, obstinate chronic diarrhoea).  
 Dose  
 - Second attenuation.
