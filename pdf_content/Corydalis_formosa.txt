 Corydalis formosa  
 (turkey-pea)  
 * Syphilitic affections.  
 * Ulcers of mouth and fauces.  
 * Cancer cachexia pronounced.  
 * Gummata and night-pains.  
 * Chronic diseases, with atony.  
 * Tongue clean, broad, and full.  
 * Tissues flabby, doughy, cold.  
 * Gastric catarrh. [Hydrast.]  
 Skin  
 - Dry, scaly scabs on face of old people.  
 - Lymphatic glands swollen.  
 Relationship  
 - Nit. ac.; Kali iod.; Fluor. ac.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture, twenty drops three times a day.
