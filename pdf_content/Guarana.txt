 Guarana  
 (guarana)  
 * Contains a large percentage of Caffein, which may explain its use as a remedy for certain 
forms os sick headache.  
 Head  
 - Intellectual excitement.  
 - Sick headache in persons who have used tea and coffee in excess.  
 - Throbbing headache after use of liquor.  
 Bowels  
 - Stools profuse, bloody, bright green; flakes inter-mixed; odorless.  
 - Cholera infantum.  
 Skin  
 - Chloasmata on temples and arms.  
 - Urticaria. [Dulc.; Apis; Chloral.]  
 Sleep  
 - Uncontrollable sleepiness and heaviness of head, with flushed face after eating.  
 Dose  
 - Must be given in material doses-fifteen to sixty grains of the powder.
