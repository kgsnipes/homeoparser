 Aquilegia vulgaris  
 (columbine)  
 * A remedy for hysteria.  
 * Globus and clayus hystericus.  
 * Women at climaxis, with vomiting of green substance, especially in the morning.  
 * Sleeplessness.  
 * Nervous trembling of body; sensitive to light and noise.  
 * Dysmenorrhoea of young girls.  
 Female  
 - Menses scanty, with dull, painful, nightly increasing pressure in the right lumbar region.  
 Dose  
 - First potency.
