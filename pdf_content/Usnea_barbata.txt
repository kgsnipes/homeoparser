 Usnea barbata  
 (tree-moss)  
 * Is a remedy in some forms of congestive headache; sunstroke.  
 Head  
 - Bursting feeling, as if temples would burst, or the eyes burst out of the sockets.  
 - Throbbing carotids.  
 Relationship  
 - Compare: Glonoine; Bellad.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture, drop doses.
