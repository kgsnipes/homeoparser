 Ampelopsis quinquefolia  
 (virginia creeper)  
 * Renal dropsies, hydrocele, and chronic hoarseness in scrofulous patients have been 
benefited by this drug.  
 * Choleric symptoms.  
 * Generally worse about 6 p.m.    
 * Dilated pupils.  
 * Left costal region sore and sensitive.  
 * Elbow joints pain, back sore.  
 * Soreness of all limbs.  
 * Vomiting, purging with tenesmus.  
 * Rumbling in abdomen.  
 Dose  
 - Second to third potency.
