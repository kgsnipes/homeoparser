 Aethiops mineralis  
 (sulph. and quicksilver, or black sulphide mercury)  
 * This preparation is of use in scrofulous affections, ophthalmia, otorrhoea, painful, irritating, 
scabby eruptions, hereditary syphilis.  
 Skin  
 - Eruptions.  
 - Favus-like, scrofulous, herpetic and eczematous.  
 Dose  
 - The lower triturations, especially the second decimal.  
 Relationship  
 - Aethiops antimonialis - (Hydrargyrum stibiato sulfuratum.) - (often more effective than the 
above in scrofulous eruptions, glandular swellings, otorrhoea and scrofulous eye affections, 
corneal ulcers. Third trituration.) Compare : Calc.; Sil.; Psorin.
