 Kousso  
 (hagenia abyssinica)  
 * A Vermifuge-Nausea and vomiting, vertigo, precordial anxiety slowing and irregular pulse, 
subdelirium and collapse.  
 * Rapid and extreme prostration.  
 * To expel tapeworm.  
 Dose  
 - 1/2 oz.  
 - Mix with warm water and let stand 15 minutes; stir well and administer.  
 - May be preceded by a little lemon juice (Merrell).  
 Relationship  
 - Compare : Mallotus-Kamala -An efficient remedy for tapeworm in 30-60 minims of 
tincture taken in cinnamon water.
