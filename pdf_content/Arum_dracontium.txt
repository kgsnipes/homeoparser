 Arum dracontium  
 (green dragon)  
 * A remedy for Pharyngitis with sore, raw and tender throat.  
 Head  
 - Heavy; shooting pain in ears, aching pain behind right ear.  
 Throat  
 - Dry, sore, worse swallowing.  
 - Raw and tender.  
 - Continued disposition to clear throat.  
 - Croupy, hoarse cough with sore throat.  
 Urinary  
 - Irresistible desire to pass urine, burns and smarts.  
 Respiratory  
 - Hoarseness; excess of mucus in larynx.  
 - Asthmatic at night.  
 - Expectoration thick, heavy.  
 Relationship  
 - Arum Italicum. (Brain-fag, with headache in occipital region.)  
 - Arum maculatum (inflammation and ulceration of mucous membranes. Nasal irritation with 
polypus).  
 Dose  
 - First potency.
