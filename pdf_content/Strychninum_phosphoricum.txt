 Strychninum phosphoricum  
 (phosphate of strychnin)  
 * This drug acts through the cerebro-spinal system upon muscles, causing twitching, 
stiffness, weakness and loss of power; upon circulation, producing irregularity of pulse, and 
upon the mind, producing lack of control, uncontrollable desire to laugh and disinclination to 
use the brain.  
 * Very irregular pulse.  
 * Tachycardia.  
 * Rapid and weak pulse.  
 * Useful in chorea, hysteria, acute asthenia after acute fevers.  
 * Symptoms worse motion, better rest and in open air.  
 * An excellent remedy in anaemia of spinal cord; paralysis; burning, aching, and weakness of 
spine; pain extends to front of chest; tenderness on pressure in mid-dorsal region; cold, 
clammy feet; hands and axillae covered with clammy perspiration.  
 * Atelectasis and break in the compensation of a hypertrophied heart; the beginning of fatty 
degeneration of the heart muscle. (Royal).  
 Dose  
 - Third trituration.
