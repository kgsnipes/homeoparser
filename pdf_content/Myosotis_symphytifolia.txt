 Myosotis symphytifolia  
 (forget-me-not)  
 * Chronic bronchitis and phthisis.  
 * Night-sweats.  
 Respiratory  
 - Cough with profuse muco-purulent expectoration, gagging and vomiting during cough; 
worse while or after eating.  
 - Bronchorrhoea.  
 - Pain in left lung (lower); painful while coughing and sensitive to percussion.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture to second potency.
