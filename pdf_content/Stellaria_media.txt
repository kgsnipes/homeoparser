 Stellaria media  
 (chickweed)  
 * Induces a condition of stasis, congestion, and sluggishness of all functions.  
 * Morning aggravation.  
 Sharp, shifting, rheumatic pains in all parts very pronounced.  
 Enlarged and inflamed gouty finger joints.  
 Head  
 - General irritability.  
 - Lassitude, indisposition to work.  
 - Smarting and burning in eyes, feel protruded.  
 - Dull, frontal headache; worse in morning and left side with sleepiness.  
 - Neck muscles stiff and sore.  
 - Eyes feel protruded.  
 Abdomen  
 - Liver engorged, swollen, with stitching pain and sensitive to pressure.  
 - Clay-colored stools.  
 - Hepatic torpor.  
 - Constipation or alternating constipation and diarrhoea.  
 Extremities  
 - Rheumatoid pains in different parts of the body.  
 - Sharp pain in small of back, over kidneys, in gluteal region, extending down thigh.  
 - Pain in shoulders and arms.  
 - Synovitis.  
 - Bruised feeling.  
 - Rheumatic pains in calves of legs.  
 Modalities  
 - Worse, mornings, warmth, tobacco.  
 - Better, evenings, cold air, motion.  
 Relationship  
 - Compare : Pulsat. (similar in rheumatism, pains shifting, worse rest, warmth; better cold 
air).  
 Dose  
 - Tincture, externally.  
 - Internally, 2x potency.
