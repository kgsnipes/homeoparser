 Nuphar luteum  
 (yellow pond-lily)  
 * Produces nervous weakness, with marked symptoms in the sexual sphere.  
 Male  
 - Complete absence of sexual desire; parts relaxed; penis retracted.  
 - Impotency, with involuntary emissions during stool, when urinating.  
 - Spermatorrhoea.  
 - Pain in testicles and penis.  
 Stool  
 - Entero-colitis.  
 - Yellow diarrhoea; worse in the morning.  
 - Diarrhoea during typhoid.  
 Relationship  
 - Compare : in sexual weakness; Agnus; Kali brom.; Lycop.; Selen.; Yohimbin.  
 - In diarrhoea : Chelid.; Gambog.; Sulph., Nymphoea odorata-Sweet Water Lily-(early 
morning diarrhoea, backache); acrid leucorrhoea, offensive ulcers; bronchorrhoea; ulcerative 
sore throat.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture to sixth potency.
