 Lamium album  
 (white nettle)  
 * Has a special affinity for female and urinary organs.  
 Headache, with backward and forward motion of head.  
 Leucorrhoea and menses too early and scanty.  
 Haemorrhoids; hard stool, with blood.  
 Sensation in urethra as though a drop of water were flowing through it.  
 Tearing in the extremities.  
 Haemoptysis.  
 Blisters on heel from slight rubbing.  
 Ulcers on heel. [Cepa.]  
 Dose  
 - Third potency.
