 Castanea vesca  
 (chestnut leaves)  
 * A useful remedy in whooping-cough, especially in the early stage, with dry, ringing, 
violent, spasmodic cough.  
 * Desire for warm drinks.  
 * Very thirsty.  
 * Loss of appetite.  
 * Diarrhoea.  
 * Thick urine.  
 Lumbago, weak back, can hardly straighten up.  
 Relationship  
 - Compare : Pertussin - Whooping-cough (when symptoms return again after being allayed).  
 - Dros.; Mephitis; Naphthal.; Ammon. brom.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture.
