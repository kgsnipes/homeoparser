 Ostrya virginica  
 (ironwood)  
 * Of great value in anaemia from malaria.  
 * Bilious conditions and intermittent fever.  
 Gastric  
 - Tongue yellow; coated at the root.  
 - Loss of appetite.  
 - Frequent nausea, with dull, frontal headache.  
 - Sickening pains.  
 Dose  
 - First to third potency.
