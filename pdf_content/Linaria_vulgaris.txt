 Linaria vulgaris  
 (toad-flax-snap dragon)  
 * Acts prominently within the domain of the pneumogastrics.  
 * Eructations, nausea, salivation, pressure on stomach.  
 * Jaundice, splenic and hepatic hypertrophy.  
 * Enteric symptoms and great drowsiness very marked.  
 * Cardiac fainting.  
 * Enuresis.  
 * Rectal symptoms.  
 * Tongue rough, dry; throat constricted.  
 * Coldness.  
 * Confusion in head.  
 * Irresistible sleepiness.  
 * Symptoms worse walking in open air.  
 Dose  
 - Third potency.
