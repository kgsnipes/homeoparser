 Cascarilla  
 (sweet bark)  
 * Acts on the digestive tract; constipation.  
 * Aversion to smell of tobacco.  
 * Inclination to vomit very marked.  
 Stomach  
 - Hunger after meals.  
 - Desire for hot drinks.  
 - Nausea and vomiting.  
 - Pain in stomach as from a shock.  
 - Pressing colic.  
 Rectum  
 - Constipation; stools hard, covered with mucus. [Graph.]  
 - Bright blood with stool.  
 - Diarrhoea alternating with hard, lumpy stool, with backache and lassitude, preceded by 
griping.  
 - Gnawing pain high up in rectum.  
 Dose  
 - First to third potency.
