 Arsenicum sulphuratum flavum  
 (yellow sulphuret of arsenic. orpiment)  
 * Needle pricks from within outwards in chest; also on forehead, right side.  
 * Sticking behind ear.  
 * Difficult respiration.  
 * Skin chafed about genitals.  
 Leucoderma and squamous syphilides.  
 Sciatica and pain around the knee.  
 Relationship  
 - Arsenic sulph. rub. (influenza with intense catarrhal symptoms, great prostration and high 
temperature, purulent discharges, psoriasis, acne, and sciatica. Chilly even before a fire. 
Itching in various parts. Pellagra.)  
 Dose  
 - Third trituration.
