 Fuligo ligni  
 (soot)  
 * Acts on glandular system, mucous membranes and obstinate ulcers, epidermis, tetters, 
eczema.  
 * Chronic irritations of mucous membranes of mouth; pruritus-vulvae; uterine haemorrhage; 
cancer, especially of scrotum-chimney sweeper's cancer; epithelial cancers; cancer of womb 
with metrorrhagia; sadness, thoughts of suicide.  
 Relationship  
 - Compare : Kreosot.  
 Dose  
 - Sixth trituration.
