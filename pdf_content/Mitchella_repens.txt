 Mitchella repens  
 (partridge-berry)  
 * Bladder symptoms accompany complaints, especially uterine congestion.  
 Urinary  
 - Irritation at neck of bladder, with urging to urinate. [Eup. purp.; Apis.]  
 - Dysuria.  
 - Catarrh of bladder.  
 Female  
 - Cervix dark red, swollen.  
 - Dysmenorrhoea and uterine haemorrhage; blood bright red.  
 Relationship  
 - Compare : Chimaph.; Senecio; Uva; Geran.; Gossyp.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture.
