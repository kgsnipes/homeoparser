 Guarea trichiloides  
 (ballwood)  
 * Eye symptoms have been verified.  
 * Chemosis and pterygium have been cured with it.  
 * Lupus of an ochre-red color.  
 Eyes  
 - Conjunctiva inflamed, swollen.  
 - Tearing pain in eyeballs; tension, forced-out feeling.  
 - Objects appear gray, upside down.  
 - Eye symptoms alternate with diminished hearing.  
 - Epiphora.  
 Head  
 - Sensation as if brain were falling forwards; as from a blow on head.  
 Respiratory  
 - Cough with sweat, pain and tightness of chest; larynx irritated.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture.
