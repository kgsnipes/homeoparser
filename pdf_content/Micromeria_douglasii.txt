 Micromeria douglasii  
 (yerba buena)  
 * A California mint-like plant acting on stomach and bowels.  
 * Used as a tea to cure colic and relieve flatulence.  
 * Is a pleasant beverage and febrifuge, blood purifyer and tonic.  
 Stomach  
 - Nausea; pain in stomach and bowels; flatulence.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture.
