 Epigea repens  
 (trailing arbutus)  
 * Chronic cystitis, with dysuria; tenesmus after micturition; muco-pus and uric-acid deposit, 
gravel, renal calculi.  
 * Fine sand in urine of a brown color.  
 * Burning in neck of bladder whilst urinating and tenesmus afterward.  
 * Pyelitis, incontinence of urine.  
 * Croaking noise and rumbling in bowels.  
 Relationship  
 - Compare : Uva, Chimaph., Lyc.; Pareira.  
 - Epigea contains Arbutin, also Formic acid.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture in 5-drop doses every three hours.
