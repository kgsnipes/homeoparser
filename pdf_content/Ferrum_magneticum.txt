 Ferrum magneticum  
 (loadstone)  
 * Marked symptoms in intestinal tract.  
 * Pain in nape of neck.  
 * Paralytic weakness.  
 * Small warts on hands.  
 Stomach  
 - During a meal, flatulence; afterwards lassitude taciturn and hot, pain in epigastrium, 
especially on breathing.  
 Abdomen  
 - Movements and grumbling in abdomen.  
 - Loose evacuations with much flatulency, especially left side with pullings in legs.  
 - Abundant and frequent emission of fetid flatus.  
 Dose  
 - Third potency.
