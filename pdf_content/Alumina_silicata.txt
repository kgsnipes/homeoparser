 Alumina silicata  
 (andalusite rock -  alumina 63, silica 37 parts)  
 * Deep acting remedy for chronic complaints of brain, spine and nerves.  
 * Constriction is a marked general symptom, also constriction of orifices.  
 * Venous distention.  
 * Weakness, especially spinal.  
 * Aching and burning in spine.  
 * Formication, numbness, pain in all limbs.  
 * Epileptiform convulsion.  
 * Coldness during pains.  
 Head  
 - Congestion of brain.  
 - Constriction of scalp.  
 - Pain in head, better heat, perspires.  
 - Pain in eyes, flickering.  
 - Frequent coryzas.  
 - Swelling and ulceration of nose.  
 Respiratory  
 - Catarrh of chest, pain, raw feeling.  
 - Feeling of great weakness in chest.  
 - Stitching pains.  
 - Spasmodic cough with purulent viscid expectoration.  
 Extremities  
 - Heaviness, jerking, numbness, aching and pains.  
 Skin  
 - Formication along course of nerves, veins feel full and distended.  
 - Sore to touch and pressure.  
 Modalities  
 - Worse, cold air, after eating, standing.  
 - Better, warmth, fasting, resting in bed.  
 Dose  
 - Higher potencies.
