 Eupatorium aromaticum  
 (pool-root)  
 * Nervous erethism; restlessness and morbid watchfulness.  
 * Hysteria and chorea.  
 * Low fevers, with extreme restlessness.  
 Aphthous disease.  
 Sore mouth in infants.  
 Vomiting of bile, pain in stomach, headache, and fever.  
 Relationship  
 - Lapsana communis-Nipple-wort - useful in sore nipples and piles.  
 - Hyosc.; Passiflor.; Hydr. mur.  
 Dose  
 - Tincture, locally, in sore mouth and sore nipples.  
 - Internally, tincture to third attenuation.
