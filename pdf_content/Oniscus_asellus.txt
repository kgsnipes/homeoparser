 Oniscus asellus  
 (wood-louse)  
 * Has distinct diuretic properties; hence its use in dropsies.  
 * Asthmatic conditions, with bronchial catarrh.  
 Head  
 - Boring pain behind right ear in mastoid process. [Caps.]  
 - Violent pulsation of arteries. [Pothos; Glonoine.]  
 - Painful pressure above the root of nose.  
 Stomach  
 - Persistent pressure in cardiac orifice.  
 - Vomiting.  
 Abdomen  
 - Distended; meteorism; very severe colic.  
 Urine  
 - Cutting, burning in urethra.  
 - Tenesmus of bladder and rectum, with absence of stool and urine.  
 Relationship  
 - Compare : Pothos. foet.; Canth.  
 Dose  
 - Sixth potency.
