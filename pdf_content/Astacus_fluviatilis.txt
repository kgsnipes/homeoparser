 Astacus fluviatilis  
 (crawfish)  
 * Skin symptoms most important.  
 * Urticaria.  
 Skin  
 - Nettle-rash over whole body.  
 - Itching.  
 - Crusta lactea, with enlarged lymphatic glands.  
 - Erysipelas, and liver affections with nettle-rash.  
 - Swelling of cervical glands.  
 - Jaundice.  
 Fever  
 - Inward chilliness; very sensitive to air, worse uncovering; violent fever, with headache.  
 Relationship  
 - Compare : Bombyx - Caterpillar - Itching of whole body. (Urticaria.)  
 - Apis; Rhus; Nat. m; Homar.  
 Dose  
 - Third to thirtieth potency.
